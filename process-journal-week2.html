Interaction Design Process

Week 1

Thursday, 2nd of August, 2018

Tutorial & Studio

In short:

1. Learned sketching and practiced sketching during the tutorial.

2. Formed groups - got into a group with Connor Meehan.

3. Completed the ‘Group Charter’ outlining the T&C’s of being in a working and functional group.

4. Read through the design brief in order to understand the task at hand.

5. Started brainstorming and begun the design process for DECO2200.

During the Studio: Connor and I began talking about what does autonomous mean to us and our community? Initially, we simply considered the face value aspects of autonomous; such as self driving vehicles and UAV’s (unmanned aerial vehicle) or in general, drones. However, as we continued to explore the concept of autonomous, we began to realise there was greater elements are divergent concepts that also had potential. From this realisation, we started to form a brainstorming session and mind mapped a large variety of different autonomous concepts aligned with different user groups we would have the opportunity to explore. Due to the large variety of ideas, our goals aligned to wishing to learn more about the user groups specifically in public transport, emergency systems (medicine), disabled/seniors, and homeless users. I will be researching the autonomous vehicle usage for rapid emergency medical aid and distributing food and clothing to Sydney’s homeless. Whilst Connor will be researching the application of autonomous vehicles to aid in the transportation of elderly and disabled citizens as well as on-demand autonomous vehicles as a solution to congestion in Sydney without trains.

Goal for next week: Meetup on Wednesday the 8th of August to go over our individual research on our assigned user groups. Work throughout the tutorial to continue our research and progressively move forward with our project. Reflection This week was very productive and allowed us to understand our wants and needs and the tasks we have assigned for ourselves. We are excited for this semester.

Week 1.2

Sunday, 5th of August, 2018

Independent Study

Understanding Assignment 1 deliverables:

  • Identify a problem scenario or a type of autonomous vehicle/drone as context for the design project.
  • Collect information on the chosen context and users - using one or more of the techniques introduced in the lectures and tutorials.
  • Develop a number of concepts based on the findings.
  • Identify accessible potential users - current users of non-autonomous systems. E.g. how such users will feel about autonomous vehicles in the future

In addition to this, we also plan to:

  • Employ a wide range of research methods + reason for why they were chosen.
  • Additional analysis through user journey maps, affinity diagrams, brainstorming, etc.
  • Use various/additional forms of representing the findings and insights from user research.
  • Persona
  • Storyboards of current situations/experience
  • Reflection and next steps
