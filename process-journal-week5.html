Interaction Design Process

Week 5

Friday, 31st of August, 2018

Independent Research

Read through Connor’s process journal in order to pick out the key insights and data that I felt was important for our users at hand, and by doing so, would also help us in finding the exact problem that we want to tackle and solve for senior citizens.
Also wrote out a design brief layout that we would be able to fill out together once collating all of the data and choosing a problem and what kind of concepts we would want to develop and come up with. Planning on talking over our findings from each other’s reports in order to find the consistencies and explore how and where we want to go further.

Week 5.2

Saturday, 1st of September, 2018

Group Meeting (Virtual) #4

Connor and I had a virtual meeting to discuss our findings and to simply have a conversation on our user group and potential problems we could tackle. We explored concepts surrounding how technology is difficult to grasp for senior citizens, and how many elderly citizens often feel burdened or even embarrassed to ask for help. We were able to conclude that senior citizens are often very prideful and aim to sustain their independence, and oftentimes this excessive independence can be considered somewhat unhealthy.
Furthermore, we discussed what we wanted focus on for the problem, staying away from solutions, and simply divulging into the problem scope entirely, and gaining greater empathy for our user group.
We will be meeting up on Wednesday to finish the design brief and finalise on a problem space.
