Interaction Design Process

Week 8

Monday, 17th of September & Tuesday, 18th of September, 2018

Independent Study

Monday:

Due to the nature of this assessment, user testing is critical in the progression of the UI whilst gaining valuable feedback from our chosen user group. Therefore, whilst I have been designing screens and iteration upon iteration, I decided to start contacting senior citizen homes in the hopes that I would be able to visit one and tets my iterations on real users and gain real feedback and insight.
However, due to the nature of policies in senior citizen homes, many have either refused or referred me to an email. In total, I have contacted 15 homes within the Sydney region including both in and out of the CBD region. I have had the opportunity to email 4 homes, while other either refused or did not pick up.
I am still waiting for replies from those that I have contacted and one I will be contacting on Wednesday as they requested me to do so.
Hopefully, I will be able to test very very soon! Testing is so very crucial and now I am starting to panic.

Tuesday:

I still haven’t heard back from anyone unfortunately, however have continued to create iterations of the screens. I have completed the first batch of iterations for the two concepts resulting in 24 screens each in completing one specific task. Based on secondary research and my own design tools, I have already been able to identify faults that I will be able to correct for a second batch of iterations before testing a paper prototype with real users. This is an opportunity for me to really refine the screens before going and testing the screens or testees.

Reflection:

  • I hope I receive contact from users soon
  • I will continue iterating on screens and experimenting with POP/Marvel prototyping in preparation for real user testing

Week 8.2

Thursday, 20th of September, 2018

Tutorial & Studio

During today’s studio, we discussed with Sophie on our progress and her suggestions on how we should test with our users our concepts. She let us know that we were right on track and that all we really needed to do was to test on our users.
I continued to call additional senior citizen homes, however they continued to refuse or asked me to email them… therefore, me and Connor decided to simply test our concepts at the same place as one another as the place he had called already accepted him to come and visit them.
During the studio, we continued to iterate upon our concepts and plan how were going to interview and test the concepts on the senior citizen users.
