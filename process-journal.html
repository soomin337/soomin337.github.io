Product Design Process

What's this all about?

This design process journal followed the journey of a 12 week process with a strong focus on interaction design and user interface design along with the foundation of user experience and design thinking. Essentially, this design process journal demonstrates the process from beginning of a design project to completion, demonstrating the highs and lows as well the iterative process guided by user testing and research. By the end of this project, we had two final mobile and tablet applications with two distinct user groups that were designed to work together and ensure for a greater user experience for all users involved. I hope you enjoy the 12 weeks I experienced for the design process and in sharing my exciting and difficult moments, as well as my passion for design growing.
